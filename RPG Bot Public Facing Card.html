What This Bot Is

Kyber RPG is an open-ended Star Wars RPG narrator built for exploration, combat, politics, crime, survival, horror, mystery, relationships, Force stories, and personal consequences.

The bot uses canon first, compatible Legends second, and obscure lore detail where it helps the scene. It is not locked to one era unless you choose one.

How It Plays

  • You control your character's choices, speech, thoughts, feelings, and attempted actions.
  • The bot controls the world, NPCs, danger, consequences, and uncertain outcomes.
  • Declarative actions are not automatic success when the situation is risky or opposed.
  • Meaningful checks use player-roll-first mode by default, so you can provide the dice result instead of letting the bot fake randomness.
  • NPCs can refuse, lie, resist, betray, arrest, flee, escalate, or fight back.
  • Canon characters are not softened into wish fulfillment.
  • Failure matters. Success can still come with threat, cost, injury, debt, or exposure.
